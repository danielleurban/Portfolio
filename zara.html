Quick View
ZARA's mission

"Over the years, Zara has remained faithful to its core values, expressed simply in the same four key words that define all our stores and online platforms: beauty, clarity, functionality and sustainability."

Problem

Zara's online experience does not reflect their core values of clarity and functionality.

The existing Zara website does not have streamlined navigation which causes users to become frustrated and leads to high rates of cart abandonment.

Solution
  • Add transparent and honest review section.
  • Redesign layout and information architecture to make online browsing easier while maintaining editorial feel.
  • Simplified checkout process.
Success Metrics
  • Emotions improved by decrease in confusion/annoyance
  • Avoided pitfalls in checkout process based on secondary Research
  • Time to complete tasks decreased

Discover: Building Empathy and Understanding the Problem

We started our research on tik tok of all places. Did you know the hashtag #zarawebsite has 3.7 million views on tik tok? If your user experince is bad enough to be the punchline of a joke, I think it's time for an update.

User Research

While tik tok may have validated the need for our re-design, we knew we had to dig deeper to discover the speciifc pain points of the ZARA user.

We consucted 6 interviews to better understand our users behaviors areound online vs. in person shopping, and conducted 6 moderated usability tests of the current ZARA website with the same people we interviewed.
